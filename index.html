FAQ

Common Questions

What sign languages does Signova support?
Currently, Signova supports American Sign Language (ASL) alphabet recognition. We are actively expanding to include BSL, ISL, and full ASL word-level translation.
Does my video data leave my device?
No. All processing happens entirely in your browser using client-side AI models. Your camera feed is never uploaded or stored anywhere.
What browsers are supported?
Signova works best on Chrome and Edge (desktop). Mobile support is in development. A modern GPU is recommended for optimal performance.
How accurate is the translation?
Our model achieves 99.2% accuracy on ASL alphabet recognition in controlled conditions. Real-world accuracy may vary based on lighting and camera quality.
Is Signova free to use?
Yes, Signova is completely free and open-source. We believe accessibility tools should be available to everyone.
